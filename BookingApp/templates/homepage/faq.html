It is very easy. At first type (enter) name of your destination/hotel including date of your stay into the blue "Search box". Than just click on red button "Search". Immediately, all the available rooms will show up including prices for whole stay (number of nights you filled previously). You can also use our filters for more specific accommodation request as price per room, star rating, hotel type, facilities etc. On the top of the page you can also sort the accommodation based on popularity, price per room, guest rating and distance from center. This is quite helpful if you do not know the destination much but wish to stay close to a certain attraction.

The extra bed/baby cots hotel policy is written in "Payment and cancellation conditions" in section "Children and Extra Bed Policy". Each accommodation has its own extra bed policy, therefore its necessary to always check it in order to find out if the extra bed will be possible or not. Please note that the price for extra beds wil not be included in the total price for accommodation. If you wish to add extra bed or baby cot, please write this request into „Request box“ in the reservation form. Please note that any type of extra bed or baby cot is upon request and has to be confirmed by the accommodation after your reservation.
